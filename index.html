GenZ Code Programming Language!!

Github Repository

This programming language was made for beginner developers who struggled understanding the terminology and syntax of traditional languages. As shown in the name, this language's target audience is Gen Z people who will already be familiar with terms that dominate this language, whether they've heard it on TikTok, Twitter, Youtube or even just out and about at school or socially, the average Gen Z person will most definitely be familiar with and comfortable with this language.

Programming Language Explained Below:

  • say("Hello World!"): Used for printing a string or value
  • gimmeMore: Addition (+)
  • takeSome: Minus (-)
  • stackUp: Multiply (*/x)
  • splitIt: Division (/)
  • itsGoood: Equal
  • itsBaaad: Not equal
  • better: Greater than
  • worse: Less than
  • moreOrTwinning: Greater than or equal
  • worseOrTwinning: Less than or equal
  • vibeWith: AND
  • either: OR
  • yea: if
  • nah: else
  • otherwise: elif
  • fr: True
  • cap: False
  • ghost: None/Null
  • leftover: Modular (%) returns the leftover of a division
  • loopy: Utilised for creating loops similar to a while loop

Sample Program

    fuel = 15
    fullTank = 100
    low = 20
    
    say("scanning fuel level...")
    
    yea fuel itsGoood fullTank:
    say("You have a full tank!!!")
    
    otherwise fuel worse low:
    say("tank is on E, find a nearby gas station quick.")
